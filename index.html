Worker Safety Violations — Gulf Region

👷 Common Safety Non-Compliances

Oil Gas & Construction sector
⛑
No PPE / Language Barrier
Many migrant workers unaware of safety rules due to language gaps. PPE not provided or not worn on-site.
☀️
Working in Extreme Heat
Midday ban ignored. Workers forced to continue in 45°C+. Heat stroke is a silent killer — often misclassified.
📵
Deaths Not Reported
HRW 2025: migrant deaths classified as "cardiac arrest" — no investigation, no compensation to families.
🏗
Scaffolding Without Safety Nets
Falls from heights = #1 cause of construction deaths. Cheap contractors skip fall arrest systems to cut costs.
⚡
Live Electrical Hazards
HRW: workers electrocuted on construction sites across Saudi Arabia. Proper lockout/tagout not implemented.
🕐
Excessive Working Hours
12–16 hour shifts common for migrant workers. Fatigue is a leading contributor to serious accidents.

Australia HSE Recommendations

✅ Priority Actions for Management

Based on Safe Work Australia 2024–2025 findings
🏗
Fall Arrest Systems — Mandatory
Falls up 71%. Every elevated worksite must have harnesses, safety nets, edge protection before work starts.
🚛
Fatigue Management Programs
42% of deaths involve vehicles. Implement digital fatigue monitoring, mandatory rest periods, telematics.
🧠
Mental Health First Response
10.5% of serious claims = mental health. EAP programs and on-site psychosocial risk assessments required.
🛢️
NOPSEMA 2024 Compliance
New regs require psychosocial incident reporting, stronger HSR roles. Operators must update safety cases now.
🌫️
Silica Dust Control
Engineered stone ban enforced 2024. Mining/construction must implement wet cutting and respiratory protection.
📊
Real-Time Incident Reporting
400+ serious claims per day. Deploy digital HSE systems — mobile reporting, instant NOPSEMA notifications.
